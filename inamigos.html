Who We Are

Not a Charity. A Practice.

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 non-profit registered under the Central Government of India, headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

We treat giving back as a daily discipline, not a one-time gesture. Six flagship projects let us go deep instead of wide — addressing hunger, illiteracy, gender inequality, animal neglect, environmental degradation, and unemployment at their roots.

Transparency isn't a tagline here — it's audited. We hold 80G & 12A tax certifications, CSR-1 registration for corporate giving, NITI Aayog affiliation, and the IAF ISO 9001:2015 quality certification.

The Work Runs on Volunteers

No donation runs without hands behind it. Here's where you'd actually fit in — and how to start this week, not someday.

🏫

Teaching & Mentorship

Run weekend classes for Bachpanshala learners, or mentor a Vikas youth cohort through job readiness.

🥘

Ground Operations

Pack and distribute meals for Sewa drives, or help coordinate Jeev animal-rescue response in your city.

🌳

Field & Environment

Lead a Prakriti plantation drive in your neighbourhood — we'll supply saplings, tools, and a how-to.

📣

Remote & Digital

Help with content, design, outreach, or Udaan's digital-literacy modules from anywhere in India.
